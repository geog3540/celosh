Projects

Public Electric Vehicle Charging Stations in Iowa Counties

This map is a proportional symbol map showing the distribution of public electric vehicle charging stations across Iowa counties. Values ranging from 1 to 12 were grouped into 4 symbols where each group, alongside a circle was twice the size as the group before. This map shows clustered symbols in areas with high population dencity like Iowa City and Des Moines Areas.

Area of Invasive Plants per Colorado County

This choropleth map was created to track the relative proportion of invasive species by normalizing the accumulation between each species residing area with the total area of the county within Colorado. Based on this map, it can be concluded that the Western part of Colorado contains the highest percentage of invasive plants.

Soil Contamination and Infant Mortality in Nevada

This project aims to provide insight into current infant mortality and Cesium levels across Nevada Counties, and areas around the Nevada Nuclear Testing Site using various chloropleth and bivariate maps.

Family Tree Movement Within the United States

This map tracked human migration movements across the united states using flow mapping which classified each movement by number of people each state by total flow using choropleth mapping. This showed heavy movement in the nothern states within the US and high flow between some midwestern states.

Number of Farmers Markets by Population in Iowa Counties

A bivariate map was created to analize and compare the number of farmers markets within each Iowan county and number of residents in that county. This map informs readers about if their county has a significant amount of farmers markets for the population and if the number is lower compared to the population.

Tracking Raptor Migration at Two Sites in Iowa

For this project we tracked various raptor species to gather insight on migration patterns at two locations in Iowa. We compared freqency of sitings to temperature and percipitation data.
